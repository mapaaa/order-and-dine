Who are we?

We are a young and ambitious team of developers, studying at the University of Bucharest. We aim to tackle real life situations and provide the most user-friendly experience one could have!

What do we offer?

Order & Dine's main goal is to provide a place where anyone can either choose for a stay-in kind of meal or, on the contrary, for some of the fanciest places in town to hang out! Doesn't matter if you're looking for a place to have brunch, or for the best pizza in town, we got you covered! Therefore, Order&Dine comes in handy for several real-life situations! Thanks to us, you don't have to keep multiple apps, because we offer everything that you want when it comes to food!
